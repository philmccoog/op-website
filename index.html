AI Exploration

For businesses curious about AI

You know AI could help — you just don't know where to start. We map your workflows, identify the highest-value opportunities, and evaluate whether off-the-shelf platforms or a custom solution is the right fit. You get a clear, jargon-free recommendation — not a sales pitch.

  • Workflow analysis and pain-point assessment
  • Commercial AI platform evaluation
  • Custom vs. off-the-shelf feasibility
  • Prioritized road map with clear next steps

AI Implementation

For businesses ready to deploy

You have a problem. You want it solved with AI. We select the right tools, configure them for your environment, and deploy — then train your team so the solution actually gets used. From document automation to intelligent data extraction, we turn AI potential into daily operational reality.

  • Tool selection with cost-benefit analysis
  • Hands-on configuration and deployment
  • Integration with your existing systems
  • Staff training and IT team handover
